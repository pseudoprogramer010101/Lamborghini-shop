Where the everyday meets dynamism

We’re united by our curiosity and energy in this dynamic environment. Here people, passion and competence meet technology, inspiration and forward thinking. This is the place where know-how and pride come together to create masterpieces. We know our success relies on the next generation, so we follow them along their educational path. We do this by investing in training and collaboration agreements with universities and high schools, cultivating key skills for tomorrow’s world of work.

IT TAKES AN INSPIRING CULTURE TO MAKE SUPER SPORTS CARS We constantly work on creating the ideal working conditions for the team in an inclusive environment. For us, diversity is a source of inspiration and we give high value to this mixture of personalities in a reciprocal exchange of experiences and skills, also through a job rotation program that improves multipotential and motivation. Our company culture is based on seeking a sense of self, accompanied by a broader search for sense of community, so that we can make a tangible impact on the present and future. The opportunities and benefits offered originate from the active listening of people, who are always at the heart of our strategy.
